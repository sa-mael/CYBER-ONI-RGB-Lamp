FAQ

Does the lamp heat up?

No. LED module with passive cooling. Safe for long sessions.

Can I use a power bank?

Yes. Requires 5V USB‑C, up to 5W.

Does it remember the last mode?

Yes. The last selected mode is stored in local storage (demo) or MCU (production device).
